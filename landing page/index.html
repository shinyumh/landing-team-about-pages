The Features

Serving as an intermediary to the Common Application portal, College Collab allows students to collect and organize all the relevant documents they need for their application and get them approved by their counselors.

Organize.

Students can add their essays, activities, standardized testing scores, transcripts, college list, and recommenders to the student's profile. College Collab helps students stay on top of the college application process by organizing the important documents and scores needed for Common Application.

Prepare.

College Collab allows students to add upcoming deadlines and keep track of them. This way, students would not miss any opportunity due to forgetting the deadline. We know the busy schedules of high school students, and we are here to help.

Connect.

Student counselors can view student progress/information any time, any where without having to schedule meetings with the students. They can also leave notes for students, reminding the students of upcoming deadlines or incomplete tasks.
